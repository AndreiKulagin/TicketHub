App Module - Device


Test steps:

Devices. Creating a new device.
Log in to the website.
                                                                                                                                                                                          
1)On the left bar choose the button devices and press it .
2)Press the green button "NEW DEVICE".
3)Fill the fields.
4)Tick "Send notification" and create ticket if device failure: and press button "submit".


Expected result:

1)Devices section is open and the button devices on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)Section creating a new device is opened, you can see title, ip, port fields.
3)The fields should be indicated by green color. 
4)The device should be created and seen in the devices section.



Devices. Add a new device with deselected checkbox "Send notification and create ticket if device failure:".
Log in to the website.

1)On the left bar choose the button devices and press it .
2)Press the green button "NEW DEVICE".
3)Fill the fields.
4)Don't select "Send notification" and create ticket if device failure: and press button "submit".


Expected result:

1)Devices section is open and the button devices on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)Section creating a new device is opened, you can see title, ip, port fields.
3)The fields should be indicated by green color.
4)The device should be created and seen in the devices section.


Devices. Find and edit existing device.
Log in to the website.

1)On the left bar choose the button devices and press it .
2)In the field SEARCH type the name of device - "NEW DEVICE" and press enter.
3)Press the yellow icon Edit on the right side of the field "NEW DEVICE".
4)Edit the title on this device on the name generated randomly.(Title must be at least 4 characters long).
And press submit.

Expected result:

1)Devices section is open and the button devices on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)In section devices you will see device with the name "NEW DEVICE".
3)Section "Edit the device" is open. You will see a note : " While you are editing the form, others cannot do this".
4)The name of device should be changed and seen in the Device section.


Devices. Find and delete existing device.
Log in to the website.

1)On the left bar choose the button devices and press it .
2)In the field SEARCH type the name of device - "new device" and press enter.
3)Press the red icon Delete on the right side of the field "new device".
4)In the message "Are you confirm delete?" select "OK".

Expected result:

1)Devices section is open and the button devices on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)In section devices you will see device with the name "new device".
3)You will see a message "Are you confirm delete "?
4)The device should disappear from device section.
