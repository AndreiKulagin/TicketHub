Test steps:

Departments. Creating a new department.
Log in to the website.

1)On the left bar chose the button departments and press it .
2)Press the green button "NEW DEPARTMENT".
3)Fill the field Title with value - NEW DEPARTMENT.
4)Press the  button "submit".


Expected result:

1)Departments section is open and the button departments on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)Section creating a new department is opened, you can see title field.
3)The filed title should be indicated by green color. 
4)The department should be created and seen in the departments section.



Departments. Add department with filling "Additional information" section.
Log in to the website.

1)On the left bar chose the button departments and press it .
2)Press the green button "NEW DEPARTMENT".
3)Fill the field Title with randomly generated value (Title must be at least 3 characters long).
4)Press Additional information section and fill all fields by randomly generated values.(email address
should contain valid value like kullagin97@gmail.com)
5)Press button submit.



Expected result:

1)Departments section is open and the button departments on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)Section creating a new department is opened, you can see title field.
3)The filed title should be indicated by green color.
4)Fields should not contain error messages.
5)The department should be created and seen in the departments section with phone and email information.



Departments. Find and edit existing department.
Log in to the website.

1)On the left bar chose the button departments and press it .
2)In the field Title type the name of the department and press filter button.
3)Choose this department by pressing on its title.
4)Press the button edit.
5)Change the title by randomly generated value (Title must be at least 3 characters long).
And press submit button.


Expected result:

1)Departments section is open and the button departments on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)This department should be seen in the department section.
3)The information of this department is open.
4)Section of department information is open.
5)The departments section is open with a changed name.



Departments. Find and delete existing department.
Log in to the website.

1)On the left bar chose the button departments and press it .
2)In the field Title type the name of the department and press filter button.
3)Press the red delete button on the right side.
4)On the message "Are you confirm delete?" choose OK.

Expected result:

1)Departments section is open and the button departments on the left-side bar indicated by orange color.
2)This department should be seen in the department section.
3)You will see a message "Are you confirm delete?".
4)The department should disappear.
